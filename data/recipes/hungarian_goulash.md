## Ingredients

- 500g beef shoulder
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 2 bell peppers, diced
- 2 medium potatoes, cubed
- 500g goulash powder (or 250ml goulash sauce mixed with 250ml water)
- 2 bay leaves
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Paprika (sweet and hot), to taste

## Method

1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add onions and cook until soft and translucent.
3. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
4. Add carrots, celery, and bell peppers, and cook for 5-7 minutes.
5. Add potatoes and stir to coat with oil.
6. Add goulash powder (or sauce mixture) and stir well.
7. Add bay leaves, salt, pepper, and paprika to taste.
8. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 2-3 hours or until meat is tender.
9. Serve with bread, boiled eggs, or paprika dumplings (if available).
