## Ingredients

- 2 cups wholemeal self-raising flour
- 125g oil
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 apple
- 1 banana
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 180C (fan-forced)
2. Slice the apple and then cut the slices into small triangular shapes. Cut the banana in half longways, then slice.
3. Collect all the dry ingredient (flour, sugar and cinnamon) in a bowl and mix well
4. Collect all wet ingredient in another bowl (milk, oil and egg) and combine well.
5. Make a well in the middle of the dry ingredients and pour the wet ingredients in. Use a wooden spoon to mix thoroughly.
6. Add the fruits to the mixture and mix well.
7. Fill the muffin cups with the mixture. (3/4 of the cup size should be fine)
8. Place on a tray and into the oven for 15-20 minutes.
9. Place muffins on a cake tray and allow to cool for 5 mins.
